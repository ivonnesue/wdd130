About Me

Marisa Edwards

I go by Reese, because no one pronounces my first name correctly.

I am originally from Arizona. But I currently live in Belleville Ilinois. I don't have any kids but I have helped my sisters raise all of theirs. I am proud to say that I am world's best Aunt. I have an assiocates degree in Culinary and a lot of credits towards degrees in Theatre and Film but, I figure its time to stop suffering from career drift and finally get me Bachelors. I am a Food and Beverage manager at a Hotel and Casino in St. Louis and I like my job most of the time.

When I'm not working or going to school. I like to spend time with my family. I love to travel, read and take my Jeep out on trail rides. Something cool about me...I am a published author. I published my first novel a couple of years ago and now I'm working on a series of children's books with my Sister.
